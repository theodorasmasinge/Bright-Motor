# Bright-Motor
# Overview of what i did
This case study focused on analyzing h car sales data to understand the key drivers of dealership performance. The goal was to uncover insights related to revenue generation, pricing behavior, sales trends, cost patterns, and regional performance, and then provide data-driven recommendations to improve profitability and operational efficiency.
# How i did the case study
Removed symbols from selling price and converted it to numeric.
Created new calculated fields: Cost_price (80% of selling price),Total_revenue (selling_price Ã— units_sold), Price categories (Low, Medium, High),Sale Month & Sale Quarter and Sale Year
# Data Analysis Using Pivot Tables
Revenue by car make and model,Price distribution by year of manufacture,Cost price trends by month and quarter,Revenue contribution by price categories,Regional (state/city) performance
# Insights found
1. Revenue by Price Category
2. Regional & Monthly Cost Price Trends
3. Low-price and medium-price vehicles generated the majority of total revenue.
